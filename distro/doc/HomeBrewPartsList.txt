Board Common Parts (Needed for all variants):
- Adafruit Feather nRF52 Bluefruit LE [nRF52832] (Link: https://www.adafruit.com/product/3406)
- I2S audio amp (Link: https://www.adafruit.com/product/3006)
- MPU6050 breakout board
- Micro-SD breakout board
- Micro-SD card (at least 80 MB/s read speed recommended)

Pixel Variant Only:
- Neopixel (ws2812) strips
- [Optonal] MOSFETs for blade power control, (Vgs of 3.3V or less recommended)

RGB Variant Only:
- 3x MOSFETs for blade color control (Vgs of 3.3V recommended for best results)

Saber Common Parts (Needed for sabers in general):
- Momentary Switch (primary switch for activation)
- 8 Ohm (>= 2W recommended) speaker
- 3.7V Li-Ion battery (15 Amp high drain-capable battery recommended for Pixel variant)
- [Recommended] Recharge port or latching kill switch
- [Optional] Momentary switch for Aux button
